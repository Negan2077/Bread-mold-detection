Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What does this project do?
A: It detects mold in food images using a trained YOLO model.
Q: How accurate is the detection?
A: Accuracy depends on training data, but YOLO models generally perform well in real-world scenarios.
Q: Do I need a powerful computer?
A: No, the system can run even on low-spec PCs, although detections will be slower on CPU.
Q: Can I use this on my phone?
A: Yes, through the web app or an API, you can send images from mobile devices for detection.
