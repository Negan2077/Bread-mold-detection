About Our Team

This project was developed by a passionate group of students and researchers interested in food safety and AI. Our goal is to create a practical solution that helps people identify mold on food quickly and reliably.

We used YOLO (You Only Look Once) object detection models, trained on images of moldy food, to build a detection system that can be accessed via a web interface. The project demonstrates the power of AI in addressing real-world problems.

Our team believes technology can help reduce food waste and improve health by making mold detection accessible to everyone.
